APGMS Accessibility Statement

APGMS is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors and strives to meet the WCAG 2.1 Level AA success criteria across our customer experiences.

Our Commitment

We design, build, and test APGMS experiences so that people with disabilities can perceive, operate, and understand our content. This includes the home page, account onboarding flows, and banking management features such as /bank-lines.

Testing and Monitoring

Accessibility checks are integrated into our continuous integration pipeline using Playwright and axe. These automated checks run on key routes to catch regressions aligned with WCAG 2.1 Level AA criteria.

Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve. Email accessibility@apgms.example if you encounter a barrier or need assistance. We aim to respond within five business days.

Additional Documentation

For more detail about our accessibility program, review the engineering documentation or the WCAG 2.1 specification.
